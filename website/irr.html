IRR Form

This is the official request form for Incident Reviews for the Grand Prix Tour league.

  • Drivers may submit requests only for incidents in which they were directly involved (being caught up in the aftermath of an incident does not count), and resulted in material time loss. Please do not submit IRRs for inconsequential contact.
  • Valid IRRs will be assigned to 3 or more Incident Review Team (IRT) members for review. Each reviewer will render their judgment independently. The result of those judgments will be posted and any penalties applied.
  • The Incident Review Team will complete reviews within 48 hours.
